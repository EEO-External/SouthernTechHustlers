    How to Book?

    Step 1: Login to Your AA Advantage Account and choose the Remote Check Option.
    Step 2: Schedule your Baggage Pickup and wait for us.

    Bag pick-up

    Our American Airlines drivers will secure and seal your bags, transport them to the airport, and check them in for your flight

    Travel light

    Travel to the airport, skip the check-in queues, and enjoy a contactless, stress-free journey. Your bags will be waiting for you on the carousel after landing.

    Book your pick-up

    Choose a pick-up slot at a time that’s convenient for you, usually the day before your flight. We’ll collect from any home, hotel or office within our service area.
